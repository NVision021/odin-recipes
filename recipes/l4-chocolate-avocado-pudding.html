Chocolate Avocado Pudding

Description

IDDSI description: Pureed

Servings: 2-4

Difficulty: Easy

Time to make: 25 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 large ripe avocadoes, peeled, pitted and cubed
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/3 cup coconut milk
  • 2 tsps vanilla extract
  • 1 pinch ground cinnamon

Steps

  1. Blend avocados, cocoa powder, brown sugar, coconut milk, vanilla extract , and cinnamon in a blender until smooth.
  2. Refrigerate pudding until chilled, approximately 30 minutes.
