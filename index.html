Odin Recipes: IDDSI Recipe Book

This is a recipe book I created as part of The Odin Project. As a speech pathology student, I have created a cookbook for people with dysphagia, a condition impacting swallowing, including a recipe from each of the IDDSI diet textures.

  • Soft and Bite Sized: Creamy Pumpkin Pasta
  • Minced and Moist: Red Lentil Dahl
  • Pureed: Chocolate Avocado Pudding
